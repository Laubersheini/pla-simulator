×

The top row and the left collum are Inputs. Click on them to change their value between one and zero

The bottom row and the righmost collum are outputs. They simply display their input.

The other nodes have two inputs on the top(x) and left(y) and to outputs on the right and the bottom
Clicking on them switches between four modes.

Mode zero simply passes the top input to the bottom output and the left input to the right output

Mode one passes the top input to the bottom and computes x or y on the right output

Mode two passes the left input trough to the right and computes x and y on the bottom output

Mode two passes the left input trough to the right and computes x and not y on the bottom output
